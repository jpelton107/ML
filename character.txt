Characters have attributes:
	Strength (5) : This determines how well they will fight, whilst in battle
	Dexterity (5) : What level of army/shielding/weapons they can use
	Speed (5) : This effects how long it takes to travel from city to city.
	Intelligence (5) : Effects how you negotiate with different people.
	Experiencee : Your Str/Dex/Speed/Int/WEAPONS,ARMOR
